About

Parveen Sea Foods Private Limited

Parveen Sea Foods Private Limited is a pioneering name in the sea food industry, with a remarkable journey that began in 2008. Founded by BurramSetty Venkata Ramana, the company's dynamic leadership is led by Managing Partner Shaik Noor Basha and bolstered by the sourcing expertise of Shaik Nazeeruddin.

Our legacy is rooted in the heart of Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh, India, where we set out to source and supply fresh, high-quality aquaculture produce. Over the past 15 years, we have evolved into a premier commercial enterprise, catering to diverse segments of society.

At the core of our brand, Prawnish, lies an unwavering commitment to excellence and sustainability. We've set new industry benchmarks in modernization and technology, earning a global reputation for providing the highest-quality shrimp products..

Our wide-ranging marine produce portfolio reflects our dedication to authenticity and quality. From Anchovy to Prawns, available in various forms, we cater to diverse preferences, all while maintaining steadfast partnerships with modern trade, retail, and wholesale segments.

Fuelled by the visionary thinking of Shaik Noor Basha, Shaik Nazeeruddin, and BurramSetty Venkata Ramana, we've expanded our horizons to venture into the B2C segment. Our mission is to supply fresh and processed marine produce directly to consumers, creating a brand image as a premier seafood processor and supplier.

Company History

A pioneer and fastest growing premier commercial enterprise, has been in sourcing and supplying seafood products for the past 15 years provisioning to various segments and strata of society. Parveen Sea Foods Private Limited has maintained its commitment in supplying processed & fresh quality - Marine produce nationally since 2008.

Transparency, social responsibility, service excellence, and integrity form the pillars of our approach. We believe in building trust with our clients, community, suppliers, and investors. With an eye on sustainability, we're dedicated to reshaping the seafood industry for the better..

In alignment with our commitment to the planet and its people, we envision a future where marine produce travels directly from the farm to the end-consumer, benefitting everyone involved. This market opportunity excites us, and we eagerly anticipate collaborations that drive positive change..

Join us in experiencing the true essence of quality seafood. Parveen Sea Foods Private Limited is more than a business; it's a journey towards excellence, sustainability, and a brighter future for the seafood industry.

Quality, Freshness, Variety, Sustainability, Reliable, Competitive, Customer-focused, Experience, Trustworthy, Efficient.

We do one’s best to accomplish our vision by sourcing & supplying the best quality seafood utilizing ultra-modern technology and processes and enriching a culture of continuous enhancement. At the same time, we value the importance of environmental management and the health of consumers. Through our long-term partnerships with Seafood farmers, wholesalers, Retailers and customers we desire to drive positive evolution throughout the seafood industry, promoting social responsibility and safeguarding a healthy and triumphant planet for generations to come.

To lead the sea food industry by supplying extraordinary processed and fresh-quality marine produce with the development of proper channel directly from Aquaculture farm to end-customer which surpasses customer expectations preserving our dedication to Eco-consciousness, moral practices and environmental stewardship.
